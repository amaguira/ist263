Adventures in Costa Rica

Adventure Highlights

Zip-Lining

Soar above lush rainforests for a thrilling view of Costa Rica’s canopies. It's an exhilarating experience and a must-do for every adventurer. Take a risk and fly high!

ATV Riding

Kick up mud and explore rugged trails with powerful ATVs through jungles, beaches, and hills. You may also get the chance to see beautiful waterfalls.

Mamon Chino

Sample Costa Rica’s exotic fruit known as Mamon Chino (rambutan) — juicy, sweet, and refreshing.
