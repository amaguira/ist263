Travel Tips & Stories

🌿 Eco-Friendly Travel

Pack biodegradable products, use reusable bottles, and stay in green-certified lodgings to preserve Costa Rica's natural beauty.

🌧️ Rainy Season in Costa Rica

May–November offers lush landscapes and fewer crowds. Don't forget your raincoat and waterproof gear!

📖 Story: My First Zip-lining Adventure

Soaring above the treetops was both thrilling and serene. The panoramic views of the rainforest were absolutely breathtaking.
