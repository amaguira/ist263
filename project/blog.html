Costa Rica Tips & Stories

🌿 Eco-Friendly Adventure

Support Costa Rica's eco-tourism by using biodegradable products and respecting wildlife while zip-lining or riding ATVs. There are multipul opportunites to buy and use biodegradeable products, while you're eating restaurants or just strolling around torist areas.

🌧️ Rainy Season Ready

Don’t skip the rainy season (May–Nov)! It’s green, magical, and less crowded. Just bring a poncho! The rainy season does get annoying when you're trying to to activities like going to the beach and jetskiing but most of the time it starts pouring for 5-10min then it stops.

📖 My First Taste of Mamon Chino

Bright red with hairy skin, but inside — sweet, white, and juicy. One bite and I was hooked during my market stroll in San José. It reminded of the inside testure of a grape. It was was super good I was hooked onto it for the rest of the trip.
