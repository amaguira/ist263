Costa Rica Tips & Stories

Travel Tips Section

🌿 Eco-Friendly Adventure

Support Costa Rica's eco-tourism by using biodegradable products and respecting wildlife while zip-lining or riding ATVs. There are multiple opportunities to buy and use biodegradable products while you're eating at restaurants or just strolling around tourist areas.

🌧️ Rainy Season Ready

Don’t skip the rainy season (May–Nov)! It’s green, magical, and less crowded. Just bring a poncho! The rainy season can get annoying when you're trying to do activities like going to the beach or jet skiing, but most of the time it only pours for 5–10 minutes and then stops.

📖 My First Taste of Mamon Chino

Bright red with hairy skin, but inside — sweet, white, and juicy. One bite and I was hooked during my market stroll in San José. It reminded me of the texture of a grape. It was super good — I was hooked for the rest of the trip!
