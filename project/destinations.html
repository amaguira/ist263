Adventures in Costa Rica

Zip-Lining

Soar above lush rainforests for a thrilling view of Costa Rica’s canopies. A must-do for every adventurer.

ATV Riding

Kick up mud and explore rugged trails with powerful ATVs through jungles, beaches, and hills.

Mamon Chino

Sample Costa Rica’s exotic fruit known as Mamon Chino (rambutan) — juicy, sweet, and refreshing.
